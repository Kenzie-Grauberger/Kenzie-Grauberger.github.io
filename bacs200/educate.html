My Favorite Ted Talks

Cell Phone Addiction by Tanner Welton Welton talks about phone addiction around the world. He mentions how we may think we don't have an addiction, but makes those who are listening realize how much we are actaully addicted to our phones. He explains why we have that addiction and how it is formed. He also mentions how it is affecting our lives everyday, like going on a date and not even talking to your date.
